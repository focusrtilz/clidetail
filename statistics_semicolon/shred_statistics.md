## Before

__The commands before `shred`:__  Execute the given command __before__ `shred`.

| Command | percentage |
|--------|--------|
| dd | 20% |
| sed | 20% |
| rm | 20% |
| echo | 20% |
| ss | 20% |



## After

__The commands after `shred`:__ Execute the given command __after__ `shred`.

| Command | Percentage | 
|-------|--------|
| ls | 50% |
| rm | 50% |
