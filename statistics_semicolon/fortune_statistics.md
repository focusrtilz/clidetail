## Before

__The commands before `fortune`:__  Execute the given command __before__ `fortune`.

| Command | percentage |
|--------|--------|
| w | 40% |
| echo | 20% |
| cal | 20% |
| fortune | 20% |



## After

__The commands after `fortune`:__ Execute the given command __after__ `fortune`.

| Command | Percentage | 
|-------|--------|
| fortune | 50% |
| sleep | 50% |
