## Before

__The commands before `last`:__ If the given command failed, execute `last`.

| Command | percentage |
|--------|--------|
| ls | 20% |
| cat | 20% |
| rm | 20% |
| w | 20% |
| last | 20% |



## After

__The commands after `last`:__ If `last` failed, execute the given command.

| Command | Percentage | 
|-------|--------|
| cat | 20% |
| less | 20% |
| w | 20% |
| ss | 20% |
| last | 20% |
