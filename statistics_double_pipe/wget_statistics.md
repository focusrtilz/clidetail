## Before

__The commands before `wget`:__ If the given command failed, execute `wget`.

| Command | percentage |
|--------|--------|
| grep | 20% |
| tail | 20% |
| wget | 20% |
| sed | 20% |
| w | 20% |



## After

__The commands after `wget`:__ If `wget` failed, execute the given command.
