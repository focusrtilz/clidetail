## Before

__The commands before `dmesg`:__ Pipe the result of the given command to `dmesg`.

| Command | percentage |
|--------|--------|
| ls | 100% |



## After

__The commands after `dmesg`:__ Pipe the result of `dmesg` to the given command.

| Command | Percentage |
